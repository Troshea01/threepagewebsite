ABOUT

Wildfire Tattoo was established in 1994 and is owned by Simon and Manuela who with the help of a brilliant team, manage the company and produce most of the finest tattoo art in South Africa.

The original Wildfire Tattoo and Piercing location was above the Purple Turtle on Greenmarket Square in central Cape Town. We are now located in Canal Walk Shopping Mall in Century City and at 4a Buiten Street in central Cape Town.

Our website contains all the information you will require when you decide that it is time to get tattooed. Explore the different artists that work with us at Wildfire Tattoo and check out examples of their tattooing in the Gallery section.

Our Events page will give you the juicy low down on any recent news or events that we are hosting or sponsoring. Please look in the FAQs section for everything from ‚Does it hurt? to Sterilization / Aftercare procedures and especially important is the section on BOOKINGS for your appointment and paying a deposit, which is essential. Dont forget to have a look at what is for sale in our merchandise section. If you are bored you could have a browse through our links and if you want to say hi or let us know about something please email us from our contacts page.

Welcome to our site and we hope you too fall in love with our beautiful world.

WHAT'S YOUR STORY?

MANUELA FROM WILDFIRE TATTOOS

Manuela is one of the co-founders of Wildfire Tattoos, which has become an institution in Cape Town since it was founded 15 years ago above the infamous Purple Turtle. It now runs two bustling branches -- one in the heart of the city and the other in the sprawling Canal Walk complex. Manuela chats to the one small seed TV crew in this episode of What's Your Story about this largely alternative, subversive art form which is gradually breaking into the mainstream. For more information on Manuela visit: www.tattoo.co.za
